    Honors


    Muhammad Sajawal pioneered a publication trend at the Pakistan Forest Institute, Peshawar.


    At the age of 22, during his M.Sc, Muhammad Sajawal became Pakistan's youngest author by publishing 14 research papers.


    Muhammad Sajawal holds the distinction of being the first individual to publish a research paper for the Research and Development Directorate of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Forest Department in Peshawar.


    Muhammad Sajawal's M.Sc research was funded by the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Wildlife Department as part of Project 10 BTTAP Wildlife Component.
